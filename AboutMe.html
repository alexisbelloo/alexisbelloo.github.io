About Me

Me!

Hi, I'm Alexis. I'm from Long Island, New York, and I am a student at the School of Engineering and Applied Sciences at the George Washington University studying computer science with a double minor in music and communications. I am the outreach chair for the Association of Computing Machinery chapter at George Washington University, and the programs chair of the Society of Black Engineers chapter at the George Washington University. In my free time I like to work on music, learn more about programming, play video games, and catch up on my favorite Marvel super heroes!

Objectives

My main objectives in my career are to go into backend development and get into a field that would involve helping people with the use of computer science. I also have an interest in someday getting into game development.
